Lab meeting

Nov 17, 2017 by Alex Quent
Tags: lab meeting schemaVR
Categories: experiments
Today I met with my supervisor and we also had lab meeting. We have discussed the difficulty to counter-balance the expectedness of the objects and their locations in a true factorial design. For instance, it is nearly impossible to pair objects so that the expected location of one object is the unexpected location of the other object. We are interested in how the expectedness of an object and its location is related to memory and whether it is possible to get a u-shaped function.
