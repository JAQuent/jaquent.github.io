Effect of SD under the null hypothesis

Apr 22, 2019 by Alex Quent
Tags: Bayesian Stats design analysis rmarkdown
Categories: general
Outset of the problem I recently discussed generating data for a power/design analysis and the following problem came up. Does the standard deviation (SD) has an influence on the test statistics (t-value, p-value, BF) under the null hypothesis? The noise in the or the SD of the data should have no effect. To understand this can look at Cohen’s D, which is the mean divided by the SD. Under the null hypothesis will always be zero because the mean is zero regardless how big the SD is.
