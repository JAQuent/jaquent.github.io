About the Diary

I will post all open lab diary entries on my blog. Some of the entries will comprise .html files generated with R Markdown. These will be featured on the blog but also be listed here. I am an advocate for open science (see here) and therefore I want to share much as possible. This should be regarded as an attempt though to live up to the ideals I have. It will take some time to develop good routines and incorporate those into my typical working day. Entries without RMarkdown files will be featured on my blog only. All open lab diary posts can be found here.
